The beloved mood-maker who lights up the room

Breed：Abyssinian

In front of the one you like, you can’t help hyping things up to make them laugh. Creating a fun atmosphere — your specialty. Sound like you?

Personality

Bright and full of charm, you make any room sparkle just by being there — a mood-maker. Like an Abyssinian who greets guests without a flinch and comes alive under attention, you love connecting with people and delighting them — a natural star. In love your expression is straight; you tell the one you like “this is fun!” with your whole body. So upbeat and social you’re sometimes read as a people-pleaser, but really you’re all service spirit, working hard for their smile. Moving on the single wish not to bore the people you’re with, that honest charm is exactly why you’re naturally loved.

In Love

Love’s motto is simply to enjoy it together. A pro at making the mood fun, you pour everything into their smile. Straight with your feelings, you hide nothing and show affection with your whole self. Great at closing distance with energy — yet secretly you ride the highs and lows of their reactions, with a love-hungry side.

When You Date

A sun of a partner who turns every day into an event. Your affection is straight and generous, so smiles come easily around you. Great at producing surprises and dates. But your wish to be noticed and loved runs strong — praise them plenty and return the “I love you” in words, and they shine all the brighter.

When You Fight

You hate a sour mood and want to make up fast — a peacekeeper. Letting feelings out makes the moment loud, but you hold no grudges. Being coldly ignored hurts most, so even when you disagree, step in with a smile and “let’s talk,” and the brightness returns in a blink.

How to Get Closer

Match their energy and brightness. They’re drawn to someone who savors fun time together at full tilt. Since their expression is straight, return affection properly in words and actions — cold shoulders are forbidden. Laugh a lot, praise a lot, give attention. The moment they feel “I can be myself and have fun with this person,” they hand over even the lonely, devoted true self behind the brightness — all of it.
