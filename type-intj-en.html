🐾 Love Pedigree 🐾 INTJ · Architect

The aloof night-watcher who sees straight through you

Breed：Norwegian Forest Cat

You never say “I like you” lightly. But honestly, you saw through them long ago — you just won’t admit it. Sound like you?

Personality

People call you cold and impossible to read, and you don’t so much as blink. But the real you simply reads people faster than anyone, catching unspoken moods and little lies with eyes like moonlit glass. That’s why cheap flattery bounces right off. You’ll turn away with a “not interested,” yet quietly watch the one you’ve chosen more closely than anyone. Your replies stay curt even after ten rewrites; you replay a throwaway comment alone at midnight. The colder you act, the more real your feelings are. That high wall isn’t distance — it’s proof that once someone is let inside, you love them with your whole soul.

In Love

Even in love, the strategist shows. You don’t warm up to just anyone; like an observer under the moon, you quietly decide “is this one real, or fake?” before you move. So once you’ve chosen someone with your whole soul, you fall deeper than even you expected.

When You Date

You’ll insist “it’s not like I like you,” yet somehow you’re always watching them from the highest spot in the room. Ice-cold in public, then suddenly purring when it’s just the two of you — that gap is lethal. You speak love through presence, not words: you catch their bad day or their lie before they say it, and slip in beside them without a sound.

When You Fight

You never raise your voice. From a little distance you return logic, calm, with those seeing eyes. When you cut your gaze away and go quiet, it means you’re watching how they’ll move. Chase, and the door closes silently — so instead of throwing emotions, leave space and wait: “what do you want to do?” Only to those who can wait and trust do you slip quietly back.

How to Get Closer

Come on strong and you’re read in a second and dropped just as fast. Respect their pride and intellect, and make them feel like an equal who can read the game with you — that’s the only way in. Flattery backfires; it’s the bottomless quiet that draws them. Show “you’re special” through steady, understated consistency, not grand words. Stay unshaken when tested, earn their trust, and one night they’ll climb into your lap and purr — a quiet declaration that, out of everyone, they chose only you.
