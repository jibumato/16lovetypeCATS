🐾 Love Pedigree 🐾 ESTJ · Executive

The reliable boss who guards the order

Breed：Maine Coon

You want to protect the one you love and plan the two of you a proper future. The kind who fires up when relied on. Sound like you?

Personality

Strong sense of duty and a knack for leading, great at bringing people together and building order — a reliable boss. Like a Maine Coon who patrols the whole house with its big frame and manages meals and territory to the letter, you guard the people and places that matter with realistic plans and action. In love the urge to “protect” and “support” runs strong, and the more you’re relied on the more you deliver. Valuing rules and principle, you can look strict — but at the root is a single-minded duty to bring stable happiness to the ones you love. Harsh in words, every action is for them — that clumsy, dependable love is your charm.

In Love

A realistic, sincere stance in love too. Over a vague relationship, you want a serious one with the future in view. You protect and support the one you love to the fullest. Bad at games, but with the reliability of doing what you say and steady stability, you win trust over time — a true achiever.

When You Date

A dependable, planning pillar who firmly supports the life for two. The comfort of realistically arranging anniversaries and future plans is unmatched. Caring, you become a full shield in your partner’s pinch. But there’s a controlling side, so respecting and entrusting their way now and then makes the relationship breathe easier.

When You Fight

You want things clear-cut, valuing “how do we solve it” over emotional arguments. Attached to being right, you can slip into a lecturing tone. Rather than countering with emotion, organize the points, and acknowledge their caring side, and they listen honestly. “Feelings matter more than rules” — once that lands, they soften right up.

How to Get Closer

A casual, stopgap attitude won’t land. Feeling like someone sincere who thinks properly about the future is the shortcut to trust. Since they fire up when relied on, lean on them honestly and thank them in words. But don’t let them run everything — show your own opinion clearly and they respect you as an equal. Notice the kindness beneath the strictness, and this reliable boss becomes an endlessly dependable ally, for you alone.
