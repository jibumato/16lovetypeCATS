🐾 Love Pedigree 🐾 ENFJ · Protagonist

The healing leader who quietly draws near and guides

Breed：Siberian

You can’t leave the one you love alone when they’re low. The more you wish for their happiness, the more you put yourself last. Sound like you?

Personality

Warm and charismatic, a great listener who draws near to a heart and naturally guides it. Like a big, gentle Siberian that quietly settles beside someone who’s down and heals just by being there, you read a partner’s emotions keenly and support with enveloping kindness. In love you wish for their happiness from the heart and cheer their growth — a devoted type. Well-liked and often relied on, you find it hard to show your own weakness or true feelings and tend to carry things alone. Beside a partner who soothes your heart as much as you soothe others’ — where you can drop your shoulders and be spoiled — you’re most fulfilled.

In Love

In love you find joy in deeply understanding and guiding a partner. Sensitive to their feelings, you draw near with plenty of warmth. You want a relationship of serious heart-connection and wish for their happiness as your own — a devoted soul. Since you give so much, someone who lets you be spoiled now and then truly puts you at ease.

When You Date

A partner made of pure warmth who understands your feelings better than anyone and quietly supports and guides you. A healing presence who gives the exact words you need when you’re down. You nurture the bond of hearts. But you tend to prioritize them and suppress yourself — so drawing out your true feelings with “what do you want?” and letting you be spoiled deepens the connection.

When You Fight

Sensitive to emotional mismatches, you fear “hearts drifting apart” more than the conflict itself, and tend to endure to keep the peace. Rather than blaming, empathize with the feeling first, then invite “let me hear your honest heart too.” Not bottling up, sharing feelings as equals, deepens the bond all the more.

How to Get Closer

Someone who takes your kindness for granted quietly drifts from you. You’re drawn to sincerity that notices your care and supports you back this time. Great at being relied on but bad at leaning, so draw out your true feelings with “don’t push yourself” and “you can lean on me.” The moment they feel safe to show weakness, the one who always heals others reveals a face that’s spoiled — for you alone.
